# Building this blog with Blazor

I chose Blazor WebAssembly for its .NET identity and the freedom to craft an interactive experience without leaving C#.

## What works well

- Reusable components with strong typing
- Straightforward integration with Markdown and JSON
- Full control over styling and interactions

## What to watch

- The initial bundle is larger than a classic static site
- SEO is easier with generators, but it is not the priority here

For a personal blog, the trade-off felt right.
