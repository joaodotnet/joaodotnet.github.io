# Welcome to my blog

Welcome to my personal corner on the internet! This space is designed for you to get to know me better, what I do, what drives me, and the things I'm passionate about.

## About Me

I'm deeply passionate about technology and constantly pushing myself to learn new things. Whether it's pursuing new certifications or mastering the latest tools, I strive to stay ahead of the curve. But learning is only half the fun, I also love sharing knowledge. Mentoring coworkers, teaching best practices, and helping the team achieve our collective goals gives me a huge sense of accomplishment. You can learn more about my background and professional journey on my [About](/about) page.

I’m addicted to good development practices and love both coding and setting up infrastructure. AI assistant tools have become a core part of my workflow, helping me stay productive and learn faster. I use GitHub Copilot extensively but also enjoy experimenting with tools like Claude Code.

Currently, I’m really into spec-driven development. It’s amazing for greenfield projects where we want to follow a plan (based on a PRD) and strictly adhere to architecture guidelines.

## Beyond Tech

When I'm not coding, I'm usually following sports, especially football (proud Benfica fan!). I'm also a big fan of motor racing (F1 and MotoGP) and cycling. On a completely different note, I'm also a dancer, specifically a latin dancer.
